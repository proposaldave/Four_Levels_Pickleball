Two kinds of pickleball.

Both are amazing. They're not the same.

Competitive
15% of all pickleball
  • Tournaments, DUPR, leagues, written scores.
  • Win and get rewarded. Lose and you're punished.
  • Ice out the weaker player.
  • Easiest path for most amateurs to win is short-rally-style.
Social
85% of all pickleball
  • Scores aren't written down.
  • The experience is in the rally and how you and your foursome feel.
4D Pickleball is for the 85%. 4D pickleball turns short-rally-play into long-rally-play — the tension and fun builds with every ball over the net, until it ends with an incredible winner or a funny error. Not caring so much about the W or the L. Social pickleball is like any social situation: creating the best possible experience for each other.
