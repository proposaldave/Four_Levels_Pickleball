Why we built this

DUPR rates how well you hit the ball. CourtReserve books the courts. Neither one knows whether you're someone people want to play with again.

That's the part nobody measures — and it's the part players actually care about. A 3.5 with great energy beats a 4.5 with dead energy every single time. The 4.5 might win the match. Nobody invites them back.

This quiz is the start of measuring it. Eleven behavior questions plus an event-fit pass — what kind of games light you up, what kills your fun, who you click with on a court. We use it to put you on courts with the people who lift you up.
